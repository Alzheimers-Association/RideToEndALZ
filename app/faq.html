  • what ride cycling route distance location
    The Ride to End ALZ is a fully supported, one-day fundraising event for novice cyclists, weekend warriors and avid enthusiasts. Choose from multiple route options that challenge you to go the distance to fight Alzheimer's.
  • location city state route austin texas tx fort collins colorado co
    The Ride will take place in Austin, Texas and Fort Collins, Colorado in the Spring of 2019.
  • when date time austin texas tx fort collins colorado co
    Visit the homepage for Austin, Texas and/or Fort Collins, Colorado for dates and details.
  • age requirement year participate participation ride
    All participants must be 18 years of age to participate.
  • long route distance option miles
    Riders will be able to choose from multiple route options, ranging from 20 - 75 miles.
  • how register registration where
    To register, visit www.alz.org/ride and click "register".
  • fundraising package benefit
    Yes, there is a required fundraising minimum for each rider. 100 percent of the dollars you raise will go toward Alzheimer's research. View our fundraising packages here.
  • cost register registration fee donation
    The registration donation is $150 for riders, $50 for crew and free for volunteers.
  • fundraising package benefit
    The most important thing you get in return is knowledge that you've done something big in the fight against Alzheimer's. Beyond that, each fundraising package comes with its own benefits. View our fundraising packages here.
  • support fundraising training concierge service
    From the moment you register until you cross the finish line, our Concierge Services team will support you.
  • support services crew volunteer
    The Ride is fully supported which means route directional arrows, police support, rest stops and sweep vehicles. Plus our amazing crew and volunteers will be cheering you along from start to finish.
  • fundraising deadline meet minimum dsp delayed self pledge
    If you have not met your required fundraising minimum by Ride day, you will voluntarily complete our Delayed Self Pledge Form (DSPF) which will allow you 30 days after the Ride to meet your fundraising minimum. If, after 30 days, you have still not met your requirement we will charge your credit card provided on the DSPF.
  • withdraw cancel registration
    Notify Ride staff immediately if you know you can't participate with us by emailing ride@alz.org.
  • cancel registration donation
    Donations are non-refundable and non-transferable. All donations go toward efforts to discover methods of treatment and prevention for Alzheimer's disease!
  • cencellation policy policies cancel
    If you need to cancel participation in Ride, please contact our Ride staff. You will not be responsible for your fundraising minimum if you do not attend the Ride.
  • opening ceremony start finish line
    Visit the homepage for Austin, Texas and/or Fort Collins, Colorado for dates and details.
  • food health services dinner lunch drink breakfast snacks beverage
    Yes. We provide a light breakfast, lunch, snacks and beverages on Ride day. We also provide dinner and drinks at the Opening Ceremony.
  • food veggie vegetarian vegan gluten
    While we cannot accomodate all food issues and sensitivities, we do provide a vegetarian meal option.
